# Getting Started

Welcome to your project hub. This is where you can track progress, view roadmaps, submit feedback, and stay updated on your project.

## Navigation

The **sidebar** on the left shows your available teams. Click any team to see its issues, projects, roadmap, and more.

![Hub overview showing teams and sidebar navigation](/docs/hub-overview.png)
*The hub overview shows all your teams with project counts and recent activity.*

- **Overview** — A summary of all teams and their recent activity
- **Team pages** — Click a team name to dive into its work

## Tabs

Each team page has tabs to organize different views:

| Tab | What it shows |
|-----|---------------|
| **Issues** | All issues across the team's projects |
| **Projects** | A list of active projects with progress |
| **Roadmap** | Timeline and board views of project plans |
| **Cycles** | Time-boxed work periods (sprints) |
| **Activity** | Recent changes and updates |

## Quick actions

If your hub has feedback forms configured, you'll find them at the bottom of the sidebar. Use these to submit bug reports, feature requests, or other feedback directly to the team.
