# Feedback & Forms

## Submitting feedback

If your hub has feedback forms enabled, you'll find them in the sidebar. Common form types include:

- **Bug reports** — Report issues you've encountered
- **Feature requests** — Suggest new features or improvements
- **General feedback** — Share any other thoughts

Click a form button in the sidebar to open it. Fill in the required fields and submit. Your feedback is sent directly to the project team.

## What happens after you submit

- You'll see a confirmation with a reference number
- Your submission is created as a tracked issue in the team's project management system
- The team can triage, prioritize, and respond to your feedback

## Viewing your submissions

Click **My Submissions** in the sidebar to see a history of everything you've submitted. This lets you track the status of your feedback over time.

## Tips

- Be specific in your descriptions — include steps to reproduce bugs
- If you have a screenshot or recording, include a link in the attachment field
- Use the reference ID field if you need to link your feedback to an external account or ticket
